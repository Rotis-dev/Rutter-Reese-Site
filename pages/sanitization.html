Sanitization Checklist

Use this before publishing any work-derived sample. When in doubt, remove it.

Remove identifiers

  • Company/customer names, site names, asset IDs, ticket IDs
  • Specific vendors/integrators if it narrows identity
  • Screenshots of proprietary tools, dashboards, SCADA screens

Generalize sensitive parameters

  • Exact setpoints, thresholds, limits, IP ranges
  • Security procedures, credentials, account roles, escalation contacts
  • Any workflow that could enable abuse

Convert “real doc” → “portfolio doc”

  • Rewrite into a generic scenario that demonstrates your structure.
  • Keep the “thinking” (decision points, verification) without exposing systems.
  • Add a footer watermark: “Sanitized portfolio sample — not operational guidance.”

Employer policy check

If your employer has a policy that forbids using internal documentation (even sanitized), do not publish it.
