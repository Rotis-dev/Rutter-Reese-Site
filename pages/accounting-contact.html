What to include

Include whatever you have — even a short note is fine. If you include these items, scoping is faster:

  1. Business type and current accounting state (catch-up vs ongoing)
  2. Preferred tools (if any)
  3. Timeline (this week / this month) and cadence (weekly/monthly)
  4. Constraints (access, approvals, confidentiality)
